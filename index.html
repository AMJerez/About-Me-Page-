Note: Don’t be deceived, we do have pollution

Allison Jerez

I grew up near Echo Park, so from a young age I was already munching on Tommy’s hamburgers and the Atol de Elote from my favorite vendor. But I moved from that place a good 7 years ago, and now live in South Central. As of now, I am a student at Bright Star Secondary Charter Academy and am starting to apply for colleges. While I am still lost on what career I want to pursue, I know one thing for sure, which is that I want to do something related to web design.
